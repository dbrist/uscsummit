David Rosenblum - Principal, Deloitte Consulting, LLP

David Rosenblum is a principal in the Los Angeles office of Deloitte Consulting LLP. He has held a variety of leadership assignments within Deloitte Consulting and is currently the Chief Operating Officer for the Strategy & Operations practice, which has 2,000 professionals. With over 30 years experience advising complex organizations on strategy development and execution, Mr. Rosenblum has worked a broad range of issues for organizations in a wide variety of industries, with particular focus for the last decade on healthcare.

In addition to his client work, Mr. Rosenblum has led or been a key advisor to multiple strategy development efforts for Deloitte and served on various governance committees. Prior to becoming the COO for Strategy & Operations, he was the National Service Line Leader for Corporate & Competitive Strategy. Mr. Rosenblum received a BA in economics from Wesleyan, where he was elected to Phi Beta Kappa, and a MBA in finance from Wharton. He served as Chairman of The Echo Foundation from 2004 to 2009 and is a member of the board of the Library Foundation of Los Angles. He is a member of the National Association of Corporate Directors and of the California Club.
