Nick Greet, Managing Director, Aon Southern California, Financial Services Group

Mr. Greet conducts strategic oversight for all executive liability lines, liaison/coordinator of FSG Resources, program and strategy development, coverage analysis, and senior Insurer relationship liaison.

Mr. Greet has been with Aon and predecessor firms for over two decades. He most recently was the Managing Director for Aon (Bermuda) Ltd. overseeing $450 million of annual premium flow. Nick also has experience broking in the Lloyds' market. Nick's primary experience is in the Directors and Officers Liability.
