Terrence Dibble, Partner, KPMG

Terrence D. Dibble was the National Partner in charge for Risk Management-Advisory Services from 2001-2009 at KPMG - New York City. Mr. Dibble lead KPMG's risk management for its $1.5 billion Advisory services practices in the U.S., comprised of Internal Audit and Risk and Compliance Services, Business Performance Services, Information Technology Advisory Services, Forensic Services, Financial Risk Management Services and Transaction Services practices.

From 1980 until 1998, Mr. Dibble was a Partner in the audit practice, with focus on venture funded and public registrants including Software, IT hardware, and commercial/industrial companies located in Southern California. He served such clients as Davidson Associates, Ticketmaster, Guess?, Activision, American Honda, QAD, Quarterdeck Office Systems, and State of the Art Software. Mr. Dibble was a Partner in advisory services practice, Contract Compliance Services, and Channel Management from 1998 to 2001. He has a BS in Accounting from USC and is a CPA in California and New York.
