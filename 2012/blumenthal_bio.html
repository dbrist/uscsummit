Judith Blumenthal, Professor, USC Marshall School of Business

Dr. Judith Blumenthal is an expert in strategic and organizational planning and implementation. Her research has been published in the Journal of Management Inquiry, Journal of Organizational Change Management, Performance Improvement Quarterly, and Human Resources Management, among others. In addition to her responsibilities at USC, she currently serves on Boards of Directors of Guess?, Inc, and the California Council on Economic Education, and is a member of the the Advisory Board of USC Radio. From 1996 to 2000, she served as Associate Dean of the Marshall School of Business, and from 2000 to 2006, as USC's Associate Vice President for Alumni Relations and Executive Director of the USC Alumni Association.

Education: PhD, MBA, USC; BA, Hunter College of the City University of New York
