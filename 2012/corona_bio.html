Anne Corona, Senior Vice President, Aon Financial Services Group

Anne Corona has worked her entire career at Aon Corporation (NYSE: AON). She currently co-leads the Financial Services Group in Los Angeles and has previously worked in San Francisco, New York and London.

Ms. Corona specializes in Directors and Officers Liability, Fiduciary Liability, Professional Liability (Errors and Omissions), Employment Practices Liability, Fidelity, new product development and other related management liability insurance products for public and private financial institutions and commercial accounts. Ms. Corona works with and provides innovative solutions to many clients such as: Avery Dennison Corporation, Chevron Corporation, Cisco Systems, Inc., Invesco, Ltd., Lazard Ltd and ON Semiconductor Corporation. She has previously worked with The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc., Chemtura Corporation and Foot Locker, Inc.

Ms. Corona is also a member of the Aon Financial Services Group - D&O Practice Group, which focuses on reviewing and creating policy language, along with providing education to the Financial Services Group nationally. She has a MBA from Colombia University specializing in finance and a BA from Vanderbilt University.
