David Rosenblum, Principal, Deloitte Consulting, LLP

David Rosenblum is a principal in the Los Angeles office of Deloitte Consulting LLP. He has held a variety of leadership assignments within Deloitte Consulting and is currently the NMD-Consulting Corporate Development. With over 30 years of experience advising complex organizations on strategy development and execution, David has worked a broad range of issues for organizations in a wide variety of industries, with particular focus for the last decade on healthcare.

In addition to his client work, David has led or been a key advisor to multiple strategy development efforts for Deloitte; led a multi-year research effort focused on the topic of business model innovation; and served on various Deloitte governance committees. Prior to becoming the NMD-Consulting Corporate Development, he was COO for the Strategy & Operations practice, as well as the National Service Line Leader for Corporate & Competitive Strategy.

David received a BA in economics from Wesleyan, where he was elected to Phi Beta Kappa, and a MBA in finance from Wharton. He served as Chairman of The Echo Foundation from 2004 to 2009; is a member of the board of directors of the Library Foundation of Los Angles and of the National Association of Corporate Directors Southern California Chapter; and is a trustee of Wesleyan University.
