Mark E. McKee, President, Capital Alternatives, LLC

Mark McKee is the Founder and President of Capital Alternatives, LLC, an advisory firm working closely with a select group of investment professionals, high net worth individuals, and entrepreneurs on private direct investments. Mr. McKee has over 25 years of executive leadership experience within asset management organizations with expertise in public equity, private equity, and hedge fund firm operations.

Mr. McKee is currently Chairman of the Board of Directors of Alter-G, Inc. He is a Director and Chairman of the Audit Committee of Hall Capital Partners LLC. Mr. McKee also serves as Vice Chairman and member of the Audit Committee of The Marine Mammal Center.

Formerly, Mr. McKee served as Chief Operating Officer and Director of SKBA Capital Management, an investment advisory firm for institutional investors, such as CalPERS and The Vanguard Group. Prior to that, Mr. McKee was Senior Managing Director and President of Pacific Corporate Group, an alternative investment advisor to large pension funds and other institutional investors.

Mr. McKee received both his undergraduate and M.B.A. degrees from Indiana University.
