Martin Kay Partner and Vice-President, IBM Global Business Services

Martin Kay is the Global Mergers and Acquisitions Center of Competency Leader.

Martin's focus is on helping clients all along the deal cycle, from M&A strategy through deal execution and post-merger integration. Leveraging insight, tools and methodologies from IBM's own highly successful M&A activities, Martin and his team have assisted many clients in transforming their businesses through acquisition and divestiture activities.

His career has spanned advisory, operating and principal investing roles, including:

  • Former Partner Bain & Company, leader in M&A, Telecom/Media/Technology Practice, and growth strategy for large corporations and private equity clients
  • 14 years of consulting experience in the US, Australia/Asia and Europe
  • 10 years' experience in private capital, focused on early stage online media and technology companies.

He received his M.A. from Oxford University and his MBA from Stanford University.
