Todd Moody, Principal

Todd is the west sub-area leader of Ernst & Young's Transaction Advisory Services practice. He has focused on valuation, strategic finance, and transactions for over 20 years. Todd's specific valuation focus areas include intellectual property, technology (developed and in process), patents, trademarks and trade names, customer contracts and relationships, MSO agreements, FCC licenses, orbital slots, proprietary processes and media content. His experience also includes business enterprise valuations and the valuation of equity interests (common and preferred stock), minority and majority business interests and privately held interests.

Todd has significant experience in media and entertainment, hospitality and gaming and has performed valuations in support of mergers and acquisitions advisory, financial reporting, restructuring and tax planning.
