Mark Collinson, Partner, CCG Investor Relations

Mr. Mark Collinson is responsible for designing and managing client investor relations programs in such industries as technology, real estate, insurance, finance, automotive and manufacturing. He brings to CCG a deep background in finance, business and economics from both the U.S. and Europe. Mr. Collinson has 18 years of experience as a banker and investor and has served as a Principal in the private equity investing business of Bankers Trust in New York, Los Angeles and London. He has co-founded two businesses, been a director of several companies, served as a chief strategist for the British Broadcasting Corporation and has professional teaching and lecturing experience in business, economics and accounting. Mr. Collinson has a Master's degree in Business Administration from the University of Chicago and an Economics degree from the London School of Economics.
