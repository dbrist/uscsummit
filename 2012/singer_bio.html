Gary Singer, Partner, O'Melveny & Myers LLP

Gary Singer is a partner in O'Melveny's Newport Beach office. He serves as Chair of the Transactions Department and as a member of the Joint Leadership Team and the Mergers & Acquisitions Practice. He practices corporate, business, and securities law, with a focus on public offerings, mergers and acquisitions, and corporate financing matters. Gary's breadth of legal experience enables him to successfully represent a broad range of interests, from family-owned businesses to large public companies.

Mr. Singer is admitted to Practice at the U.S. District Court, Central District of California. He is the former President of U.C. Irvine Chancellor's Club and a member of the Dean's Advisory Council at the University of California, Irvine School of Law. He is also the former Chair of the Orange County Bar Association, Corporate and Business Law Section.

Mr. Singer has been named as one of the "The Best Lawyers in America" by the Corporate Law, M&A Law, and Securities Law categories (2007). He was recognized as a Southern California "Super Lawyer" by Los Angeles Magazine (2005-2010). Chambers USA named him one of America's Leading Lawyers for Business for the years 2004, 2005 and 2006. Orange Coast Magazine named him as one of the "Best Corporate Lawyers in Orange County" for the years 1999, 2000 and 2001. Los Angeles Magazine named him as one of the "Top Attorneys in Southern California" in February 2010. He is also recognized as one of the "Lawdragon 500 Leading Lawyers in America" (2010).
