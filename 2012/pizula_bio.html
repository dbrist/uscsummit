Steve Pizula, Deloitte & Touche LLP, Partner

Steve Pizula graduated from USC in 1977 and joined Deloitte (then Haskins & Sells) office in Orange County, where he has spent his entire career. Steve serves as an audit partner in the firm’s Consumer and Industrial Products practice, and his clients include In N Out, Callaway Golf, Oakley, Quiksilver, South Coast Plaza and the Orange County Register.

In addition to his client service responsibilities, Steve has served on a number of the firm’s national committees, including partner admissions, audit quality, and well as a number of market focused initiatives.

Steve was President of Olive Crest Abused Children’s Foundation, and served on the executive committee of the board of directors for Orange County Business Committee for the Arts, Southern California Easter Seals, and South Coast Metro Alliance. He also co-chaired the Deloitte Endowed Chair fund raising effort. Steve and his wife Holly, also a USC graduate, have four children, one of which has graduated from USC, and one who is currently attending the university and plays on the Men’s Volleyball team.
