Norman Robert Zukis, Partner, PricewaterhouseCoopers

Bob Zukis is a partner of PricewaterhouseCoopers and a thought leader and client service executive on how technology is impacting Companies from a strategic, operational and risk perspective.

With 26 years' of experience, including 15 years in overseas markets, Mr. Zukis also has a strong global perspective on Board level issues. He has worked and lived in Japan, Hong Kong, London and Saudi Arabia.

His primary objective is to help his clients find their future with technology as their key enabler. He is currently helping Boards understand the dramatically changing risk profile being driven by social technology and cloud computing. He recently published "Boards and a Social Networking Driven Future" in NACD Directorships September edition.

Mr. Zukis earned his BBA from Texas Tech University and an MBA with honors from The University of Chicago.

Mr. Zukis is a certified public accountant in California and Texas and is also a member of National Association of Corporate Directors.

He is also on the Los Angeles Universal Preschool Corporate Council and supports the IT subcommittee of the Board at Children's Hospital Los Angeles.
