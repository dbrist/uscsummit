Casey T. Fleck, Partner, Corporate and Private Equity, Skadden

Casey T. Fleck advises clients in connection with corporate and securities laws and has extensive experience in connection with a broad range of corporate transactions, including public and private securities offerings, mergers and acquisitions, corporate restructurings, recapitalizations, tender offers and consent solicitations. He represents public and private companies, investment banks and private equity funds.

Mr. Fleck was named by the Daily Journal as one of the “Top 20 Under 40” lawyers in the state of California, one of the “40 Under 40” by M&A Advisor and one of the “Forty Under Forty” by Investment Dealers’ Digest. He also has been repeatedly selected for inclusion in Chambers USA: America’s Leading Lawyers for Business.
