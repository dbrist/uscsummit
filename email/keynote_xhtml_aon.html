About the Corporate Governance Summit

The 5th Annual USC Corporate Governance Summit brings together thought leaders from industry and academia throughout California and the United States. USC's program, teaming with Resources Global Professionals, Aon, Bingham Mc Cutchen LLP, Cornerstone Research, Duff & Phelps Latham & Watkins, and Pearl Meyer & Partners, will present corporate governance practitioners, experts from the USC Marshall School of Business and the Leventhal School of Accounting, and a wide range of speakers and panelists with hands-on experience.

The Summit is designed to give directors, officers, executives and other professionals the information they need to meet the challenges of governance today. The Summit has been approved for 13 CPE units, 8 CDE units and MCLE credit of 10.5 hours. For more information go to www.USCsummit.com.
