About the Corporate Governance Summit

The 6th Annual USC Corporate Governance Summit brings together thought leaders from industry and academia throughout the United States. Directors and officers of America's leading organizations will find USC's program practical, thorough, and a great value. The summit teams USC with Resources Global Professionals, Union Bank, Aon Corporation, Bingham McCutchen, Cornerstone Research, Latham & Watkins, National Association of Corporate Directors, O'Melveny & Myers, and Pearl Meyer & Partners. The Summit will bring business leaders together with world-class experts in all aspects of corporate governance from the USC Marshall School of Business, the Leventhal School of Accounting, USC Gould School of Law, and other leading universities.

The Summit is designed to give directors, officers, executives and other professionals the information they need to meet the challenges of governance today. The Summit has been approved for 8 CDE units, MCLE credit of 11.25 hours and 13 CPE units. For more information, please go to USCsummit.com.
