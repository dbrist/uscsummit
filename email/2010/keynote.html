About the Corporate Governance Summit

Now in its fifth year, the USC Corporate Governance Summit brings together thought leaders from industry and academia through out California and the United States. As global leaders in business education, the Marshall School of Business at the University of Southern California established the conference in the Spring of 2006 and has featured outstanding speakers such as Bob Eckert-CEO of Mattel, Christopher Cox-former SEC Chairman, Myron T. Steele-Chief Justice of the Delaware Supreme Court, and Sharon Allen-Chairman of the Board of Deloitte and Touche.

USC's program, teaming with Resources Global Professionals, Bingham Mc Cutchen LLP, Duff & Phelps and Latham & Watkins, will present Corporate Governance practitioners, experts from the USC Marshall School of Business and the Leventhal School of Accounting, and a wide range of speakers and panelists with hands-on experience. The program is designed to give directors, officers, and other professionals, executives the information they need to meet the challenges of governance today.
