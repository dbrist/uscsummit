Join the Thought Leaders in Corporate Governance

The 7th Annual Corporate Governance Summit, held at the University of Southern California, brings together thought leaders from industry and academia from throughout the United States. Directors and officers of America's leading organizations will find USC's program practical, thorough, and a great value. Held on the weekend of homecoming in sunny Southern California, the Summit teams USC with Resources Global Professionals, Union Bank, Aon Corporation, Bingham Mc Cutchen LLP, Cornerstone Research, National Association of Corporate Directors, Pearl Meyer & Partners, and O'Melveny & Meyers. The Summit will bring business leaders together with world class experts in all aspects of corporate governance including directors of major corporations and thought leaders from the USC Marshall School of Business, the Leventhal School of Accounting, USC Gould School of Law, and other leading universities.

The Summit is designed to give directors, officers, executives and other professionals the information they need to meet the challenges of governance today. The Summit has been approved for 8 CDE units, MCLE credit of 10.5 hours and 13 CPE units. For more information, please go to USCsummit.com.
